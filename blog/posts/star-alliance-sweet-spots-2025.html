Star Alliance Sweet Spots (2025 Edition)

Where to find outsized value across Star Alliance—what to book, which miles to use, and how to transfer.

Nov 7, 2025 • 9 min read

Why Star Alliance?

Star Alliance covers a huge global network (United, Lufthansa, SWISS, Air Canada, ANA, EVA, Singapore, and more). That scale means more routes and partners to exploit for sweet spots—if you know which programs to use.

Sweet spot #1: ANA roundtrip business class to Japan

ANA Mileage Club offers some of the best roundtrip pricing from North America to Japan in business class (seasonal charts). Taxes can be modest if you avoid certain partners. The catch: ANA requires roundtrip and sometimes has stricter routing rules.

  • Use when: You can plan a roundtrip and are flexible with dates.
  • Transfer from: Amex MR (often instant), sometimes others via partners.
  • Tip: Book early or monitor with alerts for rolling space.

Sweet spot #2: United or Air Canada for Europe business class

United MileagePlus has abolished close‑in fees and shows partner availability well. Air Canada Aeroplan shines with creative routing and reasonable surcharges on many partners, plus generous stopover options for 5,000 points extra on one‑way.

  • Use when: You want transparent pricing and easy partner bookings.
  • Transfer from: Chase UR to United; Amex/Chase/Citi/Capital One to Aeroplan.
  • Tip: Consider mixed‑partner itineraries and hubs with better premium availability.

Sweet spot #3: Africa routes via Star partners

Africa can be expensive with cash fares. Star partners (Ethiopian, EgyptAir, Lufthansa group, United/Aeroplan) can present value, especially if you split legs strategically and keep surcharges in check.

  • Use when: You can accept 1–2 connections for long‑haul premium cabins.
  • Transfer from: Aeroplan often offers the best mix of partners and pricing.
  • Tip: Search by segment and stitch together availability; add a 5k stopover with Aeroplan.

Other plays to consider

  • Turkish Miles&Smiles for domestic US (United) awards at attractive rates.
  • Avianca LifeMiles with frequent sales and no surcharges on many partners.
  • Singapore KrisFlyer for specific partner access and upgrades.

How to execute

  1. Search broadly: Validate availability across multiple partners (United.com, Aeroplan, LifeMiles).
  2. Compare pricing: Check surcharges and mileage required; pick the best trade‑off.
  3. Use alerts: Track your target routes and cabins to catch rolling space.
  4. Transfer and book: Only after confirming seats. Prefer instant transfers.

Transfer partners (quick reference)

  • Amex MR: ANA, Aeroplan, Avianca, Singapore (and more).
  • Chase UR: United, Aeroplan, Singapore, Virgin (for other alliances too).
  • Capital One / Citi: Multiple Star partners including Avianca and Aeroplan.
