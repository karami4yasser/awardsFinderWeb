Transatlantic targets

  • JFK → LHR•Oneworld Frequent release patterns, multiple partners, low surcharges via partners.
  • BOS/YYZ → Europe•Star Aeroplan flexibility and mixed partners help stitch complex routings.
  • ATL/JFK → CDG/AMS•SkyTeam Flying Blue Promo Rewards routinely lower cost for Biz awards.

Transpacific options

  • West Coast → Japan•Star/SkyTeam Watch ANA, JAL partners, and Virgin Atlantic sweet spots.
  • SEA/SFO → Seoul•SkyTeam/Star Consistent space via partners; check KE/OZ and alliances.

Middle East and beyond

  • US → DOH•Oneworld QR opportunities via Avios for premium cabins; mind carrier fees.
  • US → IST•Star Turkish often publishes usable partner space with wide network.

Timing patterns that matter

  • Far‑out (~330 days): initial dumps worth monitoring.
  • Rolling weekly: programs that add seats on a cadence.
  • Last‑minute (0–14 days): excellent for solo/duo premium cabins.
  • After schedule change: re‑accommodation creates booking windows.

Booking flow for success

  1. Set alerts by route, cabin, stops and monitor multiple alliances.
  2. When pinged, verify with the partner site, then transfer only what you need.
  3. Have a backup (nearby airports/dates) ready to transfer if first choice dies.
