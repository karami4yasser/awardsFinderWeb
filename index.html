Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know to get started.

What programs can I track?

Track availability across major alliances and bank transfer partners you can actually use to redeem.

How do alerts work?

Create an alert with your route, date range, cabin, and stops. We’ll notify you in real-time when matching seats open up.

Do I need to transfer points first?

No. Only transfer once you can hold or book. Awards Finder helps you time transfers and avoid stranded points.

Is there a free tier?

Browse and explore freely. Advanced features like increased alert quotas may require a paid plan in the app.
